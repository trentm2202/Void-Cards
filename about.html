ABOUT VOID CARDS
A free sci-fi card collecting and battle game

🚀 What is Void Cards?

Void Cards is a free browser-based card collecting and battle game set in a sci-fi universe. No downloads, no installs — just open your browser and play.

Collect over 100 unique cards across 5 rarities including Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, and Legendary. Each card has unique stats, special abilities, and lore descriptions.

⚔️ How to Play

Create a free account and you'll start with a small collection of cards. From there:

  • Open packs to collect new cards and grow your collection
  • Battle enemies using your cards' Power, Defense, and HP stats
  • Use energy strategically — Attack, use Special moves, or Defend
  • Complete daily quests to earn bonus gems
  • Fuse 3 copies of the same card into a powerful Foil version
  • Buy and sell cards in the Auction House
  • Build custom decks for team and deck battles

💎 Is it really free?

Yes — Void Cards is completely free to play. There are no purchases, no pay-to-win mechanics, and no premium currency walls. You earn gems by playing — winning battles, completing quests, and opening packs.

The game is supported by non-intrusive ads.

☁️ Cross-Device Cloud Saves

Your progress is automatically saved to the cloud every time you play. Sign in on any device — phone, tablet, or laptop — and your entire card collection, gems, and progress will be there waiting for you.

🌌 Series 1 & Beyond

Void Cards currently features Series 1 with 100 unique cards across 6 types: Alien, Robot, Knight, Mage, Dragon, and Beast. Series 2 is currently in development and will introduce new card types, new abilities, and new rarities.
