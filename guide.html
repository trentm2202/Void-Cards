🌌 The Basics

Void Cards is a free-to-play browser card collecting and battle game set in a sci-fi universe. You collect cards featuring Aliens, Robots, Knights, Mages, Dragons, and Beasts — each with unique stats, abilities, and lore.

Your goal is to build a powerful collection, battle enemies to earn gems, complete daily quests, and climb the ranks as a Commander. There are 100 unique cards in Series 1, spanning five rarity tiers from Common to Legendary.

All progress is saved automatically to the cloud when you create an account — so you can continue your collection from any device.

💡 FOIL CARDS

Fuse three copies of the same card to forge a Foil version. Foil cards have +20% to all stats and a golden shimmer effect. They are more powerful than any pulled card of the same type.

📊 Card Stats Explained

Every card has four core stats that determine its performance in battle.

⚔️ POWER
Attack Damage
Determines how much damage your attacks deal. Higher Power = harder hits on both normal and special attacks.
❤️ HP
Hit Points
How much damage your card can take before being knocked out. When HP hits zero, the card is defeated.
🛡️ DEFENSE
Damage Reduction
Reduces incoming damage. Especially powerful when you choose the Defend action — your full Defense stat blocks the next hit.
✨ SPECIAL
Special Multiplier
Each card has a unique special ability with its own damage multiplier. Higher multiplier = stronger specials.
⚔️ Battle System

Void Cards uses a turn-based battle system with an Energy mechanic that adds strategic depth. You can't just spam your strongest move — you have to manage energy carefully.

Energy System
You start each battle with 1 energy and gain +1 energy at the start of each of your turns, up to a maximum of 5. Different actions cost different amounts:

1
Choose your action — Attack for quick damage, Special for big damage when you have 3+ energy, or Defend to block the next hit and gain energy.
2
Enemy responds — The enemy card automatically attacks after your turn. It has a 25% chance to use its special ability.
3
Energy regenerates — You gain +1 energy at the start of each of your turns. Plan ahead — save up for a big Special to turn a fight.
4
Reduce enemy HP to zero — Winning earns you +150 💎 gems and +120 XP. Losing still earns +40 XP. You always grow stronger.
💡 PRO TIP

If you're low on HP and facing a strong enemy, use Defend to block an incoming hit and buy time to build energy for a Special. Defend is free and gives you +1 energy — it's never wasted.

🌟 PITY TIMER

If you open 20 packs without pulling a Legendary, the game guarantees a free Legendary card on your next pack — no matter which pack type you open. The counter resets after every Legendary pull.

✨ Card Fusion

Card Fusion lets you combine three identical copies of a card to forge a single Foil version. Foil cards have all the same stats as the original but with a 20% boost across Power, Defense, and HP.

Foil cards also have a distinctive golden shimmer animation and a special rainbow sweep effect that makes them stand out in your collection. They are the strongest cards you can hold.

To fuse, go to the Fusion option in the menu, select a card you own three copies of, and confirm. The three copies are consumed and the Foil card is added to your binder.

💡 STRATEGY

Don't sell every duplicate you get. If you're pulling a lot of one card, hold onto them — three copies becomes a Foil which is often worth more than selling all three separately.

🎯 Quests & Rewards

Each day you get 5 randomly assigned quests drawn from a pool of 25. Complete them to earn bonus gems. The quest pool resets every midnight and the same 5 quests are active for the whole day.

Quest types include: winning battles, opening packs, collecting cards, pulling rare cards, placing auction bids, selling cards, fusing cards, spending gems, and winning team and deck battles.

In addition to quests, you can claim a Daily Gems bonus each day. The amount scales with your login streak — 150 💎 normally, 200 💎 after 3 consecutive days, and 300 💎 after a full week.

💡 DAILY ROUTINE

Log in every day, claim your daily gems, open your free Starter Pack, then check your quests and knock out whichever ones align with what you planned to do anyway. Takes about 5 minutes and keeps your streak alive.

🔮 Shard Collector

Void Cards includes a bonus idle game called the Shard Collector. Click the Void Orb to generate shards, purchase buildings for passive income, unlock upgrades, and trigger special events like Void Storms and Stellar Novas.

The Shard Collector is connected to your Void Cards account. Every 100,000 total shards you earn unlocks +150 💎 in the main game. Every 3 Void Ascensions (prestiges) unlocks a free Rare or better card. Claim your rewards from the Profile tab in the main game.

🏪 The Shop

The Shop has three tabs. Packs sells card packs ranging from the Starter Pack (150 💎, 3 cards) to the Void Pack (600 💎, guaranteed Epic or better). Daily Cards offers five individual cards refreshing at midnight CST — there's a small daily chance of a Legendary appearing. Purchases lets you watch an ad for +150 💎 with a 30-minute cooldown, and gem bundles are coming soon.

💡 Tips for New Players

1. Claim your free daily pack every day. It costs nothing, gives you 3 cards, and guarantees at least one Uncommon. Over a week that's 21 free cards.

2. Don't sell everything. Hold duplicate cards until you have three — then fuse them into a stronger Foil version instead of selling for a few gems.

3. Use Defend wisely. The Defend action is free, blocks the next hit with your full Defense stat, AND gives you +1 energy. When in doubt, Defend.

4. Save gems for Void Packs. The Starter Pack is decent but the Void Pack (600 💎) guarantees an Epic or better every time. Saving up is almost always better than spending early.

5. Check the Auction House. Sometimes you can win cards at auction for less than the equivalent pack cost. It's worth bidding on high-rarity cards you're targeting.

6. Do the Card of the Day battle. Each day a featured card is highlighted — fight with it for bonus XP toward leveling your Commander rank.

7. Build a deck for the big rewards. Deck Battle pays 500 💎 per win — more than any other mode. Building a solid deck and learning the enemy patterns is worth the investment.

8. Play the Shard Collector. It runs passively alongside the main game. Leave it open in another tab and let your buildings generate shards while you play. Rewards stack up over time and can be claimed whenever you want.
