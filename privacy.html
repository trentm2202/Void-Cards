👋 Overview

Void Cards ("we", "us", or "our") is a free browser-based card game operated by Void Cards. This privacy policy explains what information we collect when you play, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

We take privacy seriously. We collect only what's necessary to run the game and we never sell your personal information to anyone.

📋 What We Collect

Account information: When you create an account we collect your email address and a password (stored encrypted — we never see your actual password).

Gameplay data: We save your card collection, gems, wins, losses, quests, decks, login streak, and other in-game progress so your game syncs across devices.

We do NOT collect:

  • Your real name
  • Your location or address
  • Payment information (the game is free)
  • Any data from your device beyond what's needed to run the game

🔒 How We Store Your Data

Your account and gameplay data is stored securely using Supabase, a third-party database service. Supabase stores data on encrypted servers and follows industry-standard security practices.

You can read Supabase's privacy policy at supabase.com/privacy.

📢 Advertising (Google AdSense)

Void Cards uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may collect and use data about you to show relevant ads. This includes:

  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Your IP address
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages you visit on this site

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings. You can also opt out via aboutads.info.

For more information on how Google uses data, visit policies.google.com/privacy.

🍪 Cookies

Void Cards uses cookies and browser local storage to keep you logged in and save your game progress locally. These are essential for the game to function.

Google AdSense also places cookies for advertising purposes as described above. You can control cookies through your browser settings, though disabling them may affect game functionality.

👤 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — your gameplay data is visible in your profile
  • Delete your account — go to Settings → Delete My Account to permanently delete your account and all associated data instantly
  • Opt out of ads — use the Google Ad Settings link above

If you are located in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR including the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

👶 Children's Privacy

Void Cards is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
